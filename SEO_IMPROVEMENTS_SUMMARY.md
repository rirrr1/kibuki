## How to Manage Your Google Search Appearance

### Accessing Google Search Console

1. **Visit Google Search Console**
   - Go to: https://search.google.com/search-console
   - Sign in with your Google account
   - Select your property: `mycomic-book.com`

### Preview Your Search Appearance

2. **Use URL Inspection Tool**
   - Click on "URL Inspection" in the left sidebar
   - Enter your URL: `https://www.mycomic-book.com/`
   - Click "Test Live URL" to see how Google sees your page
   - View the "Screenshot" tab to see rendered content
   - Check "More Info" for meta tags and structured data

3. **View Search Results Preview**
   - In URL Inspection results, look for "View tested page"
   - This shows how your page appears in Google search results
   - You'll see your title tag and meta description as they appear in search

### Submitting Your Updated Sitemap

4. **Submit Sitemap**
   - Go to "Sitemaps" in the left sidebar
   - Enter: `sitemap.xml`
   - Click "Submit"
   - Google will recrawl your site with the new SEO improvements

### Monitoring Performance

5. **Track Performance**
   - Click "Performance" in the left sidebar
   - Monitor clicks, impressions, CTR, and position
   - Filter by query to see which keywords are performing
   - Look for improvements in German keywords like:
     - "personalisiertes comic"
     - "comic geschenk"
     - "comic erstellen"
     - "geburtstagsgeschenk comic"

6. **Check Coverage**
   - Go to "Coverage" to ensure all pages are indexed
   - Address any errors or warnings
   - Ensure German content is properly indexed

### Rich Results Testing

7. **Test Rich Results**
   - Visit: https://search.google.com/test/rich-results
   - Enter your URL
   - Verify that FAQ, Product, and Organization schemas are detected
   - Fix any errors or warnings

### Manual Testing

8. **Test Your Meta Tags**
   - Visit your site: https://www.mycomic-book.com/
   - Switch between English and German using the language selector
   - Open browser DevTools (F12)
   - View the `<html lang="">` attribute - it should change
   - Check `<title>` and `<meta name="description">` - they should update
